# Racing Tutor - Typing Practice Racing Game for Kids

## How to Play

### Getting Started
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click your desired speed level to start racing immediately:
   - **Level 1**: Very Slow (perfect for beginners)
   - **Level 2**: Slow (learning the keys)
   - **Level 3**: Medium (balanced challenge)
   - **Level 4**: Fast (building confidence)
   - **Level 5**: Very Fast (expert level)
3. Your 2-minute race begins instantly!
4. Your car drives automatically down the road
