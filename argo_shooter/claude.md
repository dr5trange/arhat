A game for 6 to 8 years old with inspiration from roblox and minecraft. It a shoot and learn game. In
starting kids get to choose a player called Mikey who is green or JJ who is red. They show up on one
side of the screen. On the other side is other player's house with some windows, 3 at first level.
the starting weapon is a brick on the player's hand and side.



the objective of the game is

* One window will light up at a time and show a letter in big fonts inside the window. A counter from 10 to 1 will start, and the
  player needs to hit the letter within the time to score points from 10 to 5, 1 point lost for each 2 seconds. Before going to next window, player needs to hit enter.
* After all windows are broken, player levels up. Before going to next level with more windows and floors (keep adding 2 windows each level). Player is teleported to a shop
* The shop shows assortment of weapons which can be bought from earned points. Reference popular games to generate list of weapons and players able to purchase something after each level
* show animation of window breaking by corresponding weapon type
* Game ends if 3 misses happen in the same round.
